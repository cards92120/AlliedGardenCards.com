GAMES WE PLAY

MEXICAN TRAIN

Mexican Train is a popular domino game played with a set of standard double-six dominoes. It is a fun and social game that can be enjoyed by players of all ages. The game is also known by other names, such as "Trains" or "The Mexican Dominos Game."

HAND & FOOT

The Hand and Foot card game is a popular variation of the classic card game Canasta. It is typically played with four players in partnerships, but it can also be adapted for two, three, five, or six players. The game is known by different names in various regions, such as "Hand and Foot Canasta" or simply "Hand and Foot."

UNO-SPIN

Uno" is a popular and fast-paced card game played with a specially designed deck of cards. "Uno Spin" is a variation of the classic card game Uno, which incorporates a spinning wheel component to add more excitement and twists to the gameplay.
